The Facts About The Word "Race"

I don't think either side of the racial debate have any real grasp of what the term 'race' really means. How it was used in the past affects us on a sub-conscious emotional level today. The labels that we create to wound and control our enemies psychologically still have a marked affect on our minds.

Like this quote from Sex and Race Volume 1 by J.A Rodgers.

Who of European descent would be disgusted by the idea that Winston Churchill's mother may have been of 'African' Descent? Can your mind even conceive of this possibility? Do you dismiss this idea as being just more ridiculous 'Afrocentrism'? Is that feeling the 'racism' everybody is talking about?

I am not questioning how 'true' this statement is, it was said as a psychological act of war.

My question is...did it work on you?
